Inventive French country fare & fine wines are offered at this rustic bistro with an outdoor patio.

Founded in 1881, we are the oldest continually operating grocery store in California, and a destination for locals and visitors alike.

Visit Oakville
LaSalette

At home amidst the historic buildings surrounding Sonoma's landmark central plaza, LaSalette Restaurant has earned its reputation as one of Sonoma Valley's favorite restaurants since its inception in 1998.
